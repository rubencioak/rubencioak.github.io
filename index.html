Rubén Pérez-Sanz
Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona and Barcelona BGSE

Welcome to my place, I am a PhD student in the International Doctorate in Economic Analysis (IDEA) programme at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB) and the Barcelona Graduate School of Economics (BGSE) , currently working under the supervision of Joan Llull .

My research interests are focused on Labour Markets and (Structural) Microeconometrics . Particularly working unions and collective agreements using Social Security data from Spain.

Currently, I am researching how colletively agreed minimum wages decided by a minority of agents - probably in their interests - moulds the decision of firms to hire and workers to be up for hiring.
